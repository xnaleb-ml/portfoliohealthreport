## Executive Summary: Report Export Feature

The Report Export Feature is currently flagged as **At Risk**, driven by an unresolved scope change request that requires prompt leadership attention. Specifically, the client has requested the addition of a CSV export option for monthly reports — a capability that falls outside the original project scope. While the Business Analyst has confirmed the technical feasibility of this enhancement, no formal effort assessment has been completed, and the critical question of whether this addition can be accommodated within the current sprint schedule remains unanswered. The request has been escalated from the Account Manager to the Project Manager, but as of June 25, 2025, no decision or response has been recorded.

If left unaddressed, this open item carries meaningful risk to both schedule integrity and client expectations. Without a clear and timely response from the Project Manager, the client may assume the feature is being incorporated, creating potential misalignment on deliverables and timeline commitments. It is recommended that the Project Manager prioritize a formal scope assessment and communicate a definitive decision to the Account Manager as soon as possible. Should the team elect to include the CSV export functionality, appropriate sprint planning adjustments and stakeholder alignment should follow immediately to prevent downstream delays.
