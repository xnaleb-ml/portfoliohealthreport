## Executive Summary: Divatkirály Webshop — Project Health Update

The Divatkirály Webshop project is currently flagged as **At Risk**, with one unresolved action item stemming from a stakeholder misalignment identified on May 26, 2025. Specifically, the client's feedback requesting that the 'Add to Cart' button be made more prominent has not been sufficiently defined, leaving the development team without clear direction on whether the requested change pertains to the button's color, its size, or its visual weight. Although the Business Analyst appropriately escalated this ambiguity and the Project Manager assigned the clarification task to the Client Relationship Manager, no confirmation of resolution has been recorded, meaning this requirement remains open and unactionable.

While this issue is currently assessed at medium severity, its continued lack of resolution poses a tangible risk to delivery timelines and design consistency. A core conversion element of the webshop — the primary call-to-action button — cannot be finalized without explicit client sign-off, and any assumptions made in the interim could result in rework and eroded client confidence. It is recommended that the Client Relationship Manager prioritize obtaining written confirmation of the client's intent at the earliest opportunity, and that the Project Manager establish a clear deadline for this clarification to prevent further downstream delays.
